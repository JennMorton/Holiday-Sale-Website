Studio Holiday Sale Gallery The Clay Guild

The annual ceramic Holiday Sale is a great opportunity for both students and the public. Students experience networking, showing and selling their own work while the public browses one-of-a-kind pieces! It's a great place for a quick stop after school or work. Warm and cozy studio with light refreshments, hors d'oeuvre, holiday festivity and friends. It's a nice pick-me-up after a long day.

You can learn more about the Mary Schiller Myers School of Art and other sales the school holds at the school's homepage.
